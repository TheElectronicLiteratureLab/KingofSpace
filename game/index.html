    Rules of the Kitchen

    • As things get further away from the floor, they get lighter. Water vapor will rise and not fall again.
    • The effects of gravity change depending on the direction you walk in. If you walk in the direction of the rotation, apparent gravity is higher.
    • Directionality is dependent on the movement of the kitchen. To get to any placein the kitchen, you must move in a clockwise spiral when you are going in one direction and a counterclockwise spiral in the other.
    • Clean up your messes.
    • Wash hands after eating.
    • Be careful of the mirrors.
    • Enjoy the pie
