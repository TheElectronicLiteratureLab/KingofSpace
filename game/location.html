Greatship

The Hermes-Eccentric greatships resemble the wagon trains of the old American frontiers. A highly intelligent central computer forms the nucleus of a system of cargo pods and independent ships, traveling in matched orbits and linked by horizators. Central control provides orbital matching services, theft and sabotage prevention, and intelligent defense systems.
